Equivariant Structured Positional Rotations (STRING): Robustness Monolith
======================================================================
This document aggregates all necessary proofs for the two key robustness results of STRING:
1. FRAGILITY: Relaxing commutativity constraints leads to quadratic error growth (Tier 5).
2. ZERO-GAP: Exact STRING constraints guarantee zero invariant residual (Tier 8').

Note: Independently defined "ESPR" models are removed. "Relaxed STRING" is analyzed solely via perturbation bounds.
